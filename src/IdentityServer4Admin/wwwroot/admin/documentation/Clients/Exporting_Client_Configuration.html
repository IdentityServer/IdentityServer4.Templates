Exporting Clients

  • -Export All

You can export your Client configuration within AdminUI to a .json file, this can be used to move your Client configuration between your enviroments.

Export All

To export all your client configuration within AdminUI, navigate to the Clients page and click the 'Export All' button.

This will also export all connected IdentityResources, ProtectedResources and ClaimTypes that are linked to your Clients.
